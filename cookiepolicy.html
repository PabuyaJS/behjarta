For details on how we process your personal data concerning Cookies, refer to our Privacy Policy


What are Cookies?

Cookies are text files sent from the Webpage, stored on your device (computer, phone, etc.). They allow us to recognize your device on subsequent visits, provide essential functions, and monitor user behavior on the Webpage.


Duration of Cookie Storage:

There are two types of Cookies: Session cookies (deleted upon browser close) and Permanent cookies (remain on your device until indicated expiry or removal).


Behjärta's Use of Cookies:

We use the following types of Cookies:


  • - Performance: Analyzes Webpage usage and monitors performance.
  • - Operation and Optimization: Ensures proper Webpage functionality and provides insights for improvement.
  • - Marketing: Tailors marketing content to user preferences.
  • - Security: Enhances security by preventing, detecting, and managing harmful activities.
