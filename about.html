We are Behjärta


Simplifying Success with Heartfelt Care

Founded in 2019 as MS Secure Solutions AB in the dynamic city of Stockholm, Behjärta has grown into a symbol of innovation and efficiency in the realm of business solutions.

Our journey began with a commitment to providing secure solutions, evolving over time to specialize in Google Workspace and Microsoft Office automation, data sorting, customized dashboard creations, connectors to other services, web design, and more.


Why Behjärta?


At the core of our identity is the essence of our name. "Behjärta" in Swedish encompasses the idea of being close to the heart, caring for.

This name was chosen with great purpose, reflecting our passion for alleviating the complexities that businesses face in their day-to-day operations.

Our Philosophy: A Heartfelt Commitment

Behjärta embodies more than just a service provider. It represents a sincere dedication to seeing our clients succeed in every endeavor.
We understand the challenges businesses encounter, and our goal is to make their journey at least a bit easier.

Our approach is not just about providing solutions; it's about fostering a partnership rooted in understanding, support, and a genuine desire to contribute to your success.
